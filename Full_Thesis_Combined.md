## Summary of Key Findings

1. 88.5% of batches experienced course completion delays (average 9.08 days)
2. Faculty Load Score (FLS) is the dominant predictor of delay (r = 0.847, R2 = 0.9094 in interaction model)
3. Batch Pressure threshold effect identified at BP = 1.05
4. Interaction between FLS and BP is multiplicative: worst case (High FLS + High BP) = 15.40 days delay vs best case 4.92 days
5. La Excellence outperforms due to lowest FLS (122.86) despite moderate-to-high batch pressure

## Key Recommendations

1. Faculty Load Score ceiling: FLS less than or equal to 150
2. Batch Pressure cap: BP less than or equal to 1.10
3. Operational Risk Score: ORS = BP x FLS, flag when ORS greater than 200
4. Dynamic enrollment caps based on current FLS levels
5. Benchmark against La Excellence faculty deployment model
